Check out our Rivers!

Salmon River - Idaho

The Salmon River rafting experience offers a stunning journey through the Idaho wilderness. Featuring intermediate rapids, slower portions to take a refreshing dip, and the occasional wildlife such as otters and bighorn sheep.

Snake River - Idaho

Hugging the Oregon-Idaho border, the Snake River rafting experience offers unique scenery and the beautiful Hell's Canyon. With mountains to admire, exciting rapids, and beautiful wildlife including bears, eagles, and mountain goats, what are you waiting for?

Owyhee River - Oregon

A tributary to the Snake River, this river has carved stunning canyons running through Oregon. Offering stunning views of beautifully colored canyons, a large variety of wildlife, and even hot springs, this is certainly a journey to be appreciated.
